***Version 1.5 - RETURN TO ZOOTOPIA:***
*Additions:*
    -Added Pawbert character.

*Changes*
    -Updated to 1.21.11
    -Many sound effects now have variated pitches.
    -Gumball's ability now plays different particles when he is in delay, and plays a new sound and particle when delay ends.

*Balancing Changes*
    -Increased level of poison from Springtrap's ability. (Poison I -> II).
    -Decreased Gumball's ability delay. (20t -> 15t).
    -Health now *properly* scales with size.
        -Small size (14 HP -> 18 HP).
        -Smaller size (14 HP -> 16 HP).
        -Tiny remains at 14 HP.

*Bug Fixes*
    -Fixed bug where Zombie could lose his weapon forever if his grave was down.
    -Fixed bug where Beetlejuice's ability would announce spawn rolls during his ability.

*Internal Changes*
    -Updated format to "94.1"
    -All tools are now included in the "du-in:weapons" item tag.
    -Added "#minecraft:spears" to the "du-in:weapons" item tag.
    -Added "minecraft:blaze_rod" to the "du-in:secondary" item tag.
    -Updated Gamerules to follow new format.
    -Moved asgore trident functions into the "projectiles" function.
    -Merged the two night ambience check functions from "general" into a new function.
    -Removed "played" tag in service of simply using the "player" score.
    -Submodes are now stored as a score in a fake player and tested, rather than testing tags on all players.
    -Main gamemodes are now tracked via "gamemode" score in "#main", rather than testing tags on all players.
    -Spam clicking submode is now run through fake player score rather than a tag.
    -Easter Egg character music is now run through a fake player score.
    -Armor return system now runs once a second rather than every tick.

*Resourcepack Changes*
