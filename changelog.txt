*Balancing Changes*
    -Increased level of poison from Springtrap's ability. (Poison I -> II).
    -Decreased Gumball's ability delay. (20t -> 15t).
    -Reduced Jevil's ability range. (10 blocks -> 4 blocks).
    -Reduced Skeleton's ability cooldown between modes. (150 -> 100).
    -Health now *properly* scales with size.
        -Small size (14 HP -> 18 HP).
        -Smaller size (14 HP -> 16 HP).
        -Tiny remains at 14 HP.
    -Air Drag and Friction now changes based off of character size.
        -Tiny - Air Drag 0.95, Friction 1.15
        -Smaller - Air Drag: 0.9, Friction: 1.1
        -Small - Air Drag: 0.85, Friction: 1.05
        -Medium - Air Drag: 0.8, Friction: 1.0
        -Big - Air Drag: 0.75, Friction: 0.95
        -Bigger - Air Drag: 0.7, Friction: 0.9
        -Huge - Air Drag: 0.65, Friction: 0.85
